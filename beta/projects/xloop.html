Services

Objective

To come up with an inspirational grid that could communicate the visual/interactive feel of the website. Also, improving the existing design, vision/strategy for the website along with brand identity.

Solution

I started with the design of the logo and tried something different. I transformed the old visual identity into a much bolder style. Combined with the strategy and the other visual elements, I set the foundation for the new website.
