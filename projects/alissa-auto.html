Objective

There was a need of an interactive web portal and mobile APP that would provide customers to view and order the Jac Motors online and also their spare parts.

Solution

The solution involved creating a portal accessible by multiple internal teams, their customers and their partners.

Mobile APP

The priority is complete solution designs that are mobile app redesign to handheld devices. All deliverables are created and deployed to meet the unique demands of mobile customers. There was a clear focus on usability and efficiency to improve mobile interactive experiences.
